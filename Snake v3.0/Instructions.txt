1.Menu Controls
a)Main menu;
-Move trough the main menu's options by clicking either Up arrow or Down arrow
-Choose an option by pressing Enter
b)Options menu;
-Choose starting speed or color by moving trough them by clicking Left arrow or Right arrow
-Choose what option to change by using the Up Arrow and Down Arrow
-Submit your choice by pressing Enter
2.Movement
a)Move the snake's directions by pressing one of the arrows;
b)The snake's speed increases with its size(like the original game);
c)If you "bite your tail" or hit the borders the game will end;
d)When the game ends your score will be shown to you. The score depends on the ammount of food your snake ate
  and the level(speed) at which you were playing.
