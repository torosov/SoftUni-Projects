1.Menu Controls
a)Main menu;
-Move trough the main menu's options by clicking either Up arrow or Down arrow
-Choose an option by pressing Enter
b)Options menu;
-Choose starting speed by moving trough them by clicking Left arrow or Right arrow
-Submit your choice by pressing Enter
2.Movement
a)Move the snake's directions by pressing one of the arrows;
b)The snake's speed increases with its size(like the original game);
c)If you "bite your tail" or hit the borders the game will end;
d)When the game ends it will show your score which is calculated by the ammount of stars you have on your tail*10
